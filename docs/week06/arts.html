ARTS

The art (from the Latin ars , artis , and this of the Greek τέχνη Techne ) 1 is generally understood as any activity or product made with a purpose aesthetic and also communicative through which express thoughts , emotions and, in general, a vision of the world , through various resources, such as plastic , linguistic , sound , bodily and mixed. 2 Art is a component of culture, reflecting in its conception the economic and social bases , and the transmission of ideas and values, inherent to any human culture throughout space and time. It is usually considered that with the appearance of Homo sapiens , art initially had a ritual , magical or religious function ( Palaeolithic art ), but that function changed with the evolution of the human being, acquiring an aesthetic component and a social, pedagogical , commercial function. or simply ornamental.

The notion of art continues to be subject to deep disputes, since its definition is open to multiple interpretations, which vary according to the culture, the time, the movement, or the society for which the term has a certain meaning. The word 'art' has a wide meaning, being able to designate any human activity done with care and dedication, or any set of rules necessary to optimally develop an activity: this is how we speak of " culinary art ", " medical art ", " martial arts ”,“ drag arts ”in fishing , and so on. In that sense, art is synonymousof ability, ability, talent, experience. However, more commonly art is considered as a creative activity of the human being, by which it produces a series of objects ( works of art ) that are unique, and whose purpose is mainly aesthetic.
