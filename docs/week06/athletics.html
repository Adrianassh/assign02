Athletics

Alexander Quintana Rojas ODB ( Tunja , Boyaca , Colombia , 4 of February of 1990 ), better known as nairo Quintana , is a road cyclist Colombian professional since 2009 and current member of the French team Arkéa Samsic , category UCI ProTeam . During his professional career he has raced in the Boyacá es para Vivirla , Café de Colombia / Colombia es Pasión and Movistar Team teams
In 2014 he made history for Colombian cycling , and the most important feat of his sports career, when he was crowned the winner in the Giro d'Italia , being the first Colombian and Latin American to obtain this title, and the second, after Lucho Herrera , in win one of the three Grand Tours. In 2016 he achieves his most outstanding season of his sports career, since during the season he achieves important triumphs such as winning the Tour of Catalonia , the Tour of Romandie , the Southern Route , he again repeats podium in third place in the Tour de France , and he finally achieves his second great victory winning the Vuelta a España over his most important rival Chris Froome , a podium that is also completed by the Colombian Esteban Chaves .


Policarpa Salavarrieta university soccer team has been for many years one of the most recognized nationally. The youth of the team have participated in different national and international championships, of those championships, they won the inter-university championship of the city of Bogota in 2016, this triumph led them to participate in the national university championship, there they remained as runners-up in front of the National University, however, and despite not obtaining the title, they were able to attend the South American college football championship. There after a great fight, along with training, good nutrition, they managed to be champions in this tournament.


The girls of our university are not far behind, the women's ballroom football team has stood out more than any other team at the university. They are warrior girls, who fight both academically and sports. They have won many titles in different championships, even more than the boys, but the most admirable is the social work they started, helping low-income girls to access higher education through sports. Our university is called Policarpa salavarrieta, a key woman in the freedom of our country, Colombia, this female team represents the entire legacy that Policarpa left, especially the defense and generation of new opportunities for women.


Sports, for many it is just a game, for us the Policarpa Salavarrieta University is more than that. Sports means health, teamwork, constant effort, love for sports, self-love, all this together with academic training, makes our university students feel that their lifestyle can have a balance, have the mind and the body healthy. Within any of the programs, students have the option of being part of the sports world of the university, and choose the one that they feel most comfortable in and that fits their academic schedules.
